ABOUT ME

Hello! I’m Ema, an architecture student with a minor in real estate development and construction project management, pursuing my Bachelor’s degree at the University of Maryland.

My work focuses on creating sustainable, functional, and aesthetic spaces that unite communities and promote a healthier future. With my educational experience in architecture and professional experience in project management and real estate, I bridge the gap between concept and execution.

My portfolio reflects my commitment to sustainable design, intention, precision, and innovative solutions for modern challenges.
